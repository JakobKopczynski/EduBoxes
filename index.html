Welcome

Welcome to Educational Boxes that deal with the topic "New perspectives on culture and their consequence for intercultural business."

What can You expect? 


The main topic this Open Educational Resources deal with is "intercultural communication and cross-cultural management". It basicly consists of 3 lesson units: social relations and networks in the context of globalisation, social relations towards a network approach and Guanxi or networking in China today. The elements are designed in order to be used in Your own courses. If You would like to spread Your own versions of existing parts or Your own matrial, please contact us.
